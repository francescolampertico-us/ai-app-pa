# Eval Case 01: State-level energy pitch

## Input
```
--issue "renewable energy tax credits"
--location "Texas"
--media-types "mainstream,broadcast,trade"
--num-contacts 10
```

## Expected
- Contacts include Texas-based outlets (Houston Chronicle, Texas Tribune, etc.)
- Media types distributed across mainstream, broadcast, and trade
- Pitch angles are Texas-specific, not generic national angles
- Email addresses follow outlet patterns
- Previous coverage field populated for at least some contacts
- All required columns present in Excel output
