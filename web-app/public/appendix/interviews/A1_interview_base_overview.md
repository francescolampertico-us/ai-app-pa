## Purpose of the Interviews

The interviews were used to understand:

- how Public Affairs professionals currently organize day-to-day work
- where Generative AI is already being used in practice
- which tasks appear most suitable for AI support
- where trust, accuracy, confidentiality, and judgment limit adoption
- what future applications practitioners consider promising

## Supplementary Notes

In addition to the formal interview base, the project was informed by supplementary field notes drawn from:

- informal conversations with Public Affairs professionals
- classroom discussions and guest lectures
- conversations with professors, colleagues, and peers
- direct exposure to work in the field through internship and capstone development

These supplementary notes informed the framing and orientation of the project, but were kept distinct from the formal interview evidence.
